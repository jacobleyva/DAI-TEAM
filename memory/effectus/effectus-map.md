# EFFECTUS Map

> **EFFECTUS is the team's substrate of intent.** `memory/identity.md` answers *who we are*. EFFECTUS answers *what we are pursuing, what we believe, what we have learned, what gets in the way, how we move, and how we model the world.* It is the leverage layer beneath identity.

## Why EFFECTUS Is a Folder, Not a File

A single flat `memory/identity.md` works for an individual but loses leverage at team scale. EFFECTUS gets seven seed files because each one answers a different question and changes on a different cadence. Compressing them into one note buries the leverage — and Codex has no way to surface what is buried.

## The Seven Files

| File | What it holds | Cadence |
|------|--------------|---------|
| [`mission.md`](./mission.md) | Long-arc purpose. What the team is for. Rarely changes. | Yearly or after major pivots |
| [`goals.md`](./goals.md) | Active, dated goals. The current quarter or year's commitments. | Quarterly review |
| [`beliefs.md`](./beliefs.md) | Worldview and frames the team operates from. Strong positions. | Yearly; updated when refuted |
| [`wisdom.md`](./wisdom.md) | Hard-won lessons. One-line operating rules earned from past pain. | Whenever earned |
| [`challenges.md`](./challenges.md) | Active impediments. What is in the way right now. | Monthly review |
| [`strategies.md`](./strategies.md) | How the team intends to pursue the goals. The route, not the destination. | Per-goal; revised when goals shift |
| [`models.md`](./models.md) | Mental models the team uses to make sense of its domain. | Append as new frames prove useful |

## How to Use This

- **At session start (read implicitly via `memory/session-context.md`):** identity tells you who; current-focus tells you what's hot right now. EFFECTUS files are read on-demand when leverage matters.
- **At goal-review (quarterly):** read all seven. Update `goals.md` and `challenges.md`. Reassess whether `strategies.md` still serves `mission.md`.
- **When making a non-trivial decision:** open `beliefs.md` and `models.md`. If a decision contradicts a stated belief, that is a stop-the-line moment.
- **When something hurts:** capture the lesson in `wisdom.md`. One sentence. Operate-able. "We do X because Y burned us when we didn't."

## Filling the Seeds

Each of the seven files ships with frontmatter, a brief explanation of what belongs there, and `(SEED)` example entries. Replace the seeds with the team's real content. Keep the structure.

## EFFECTUS and `memory/identity.md`

`memory/identity.md` is the lightweight, single-paragraph view that loads at session start. EFFECTUS is the deeper substrate beneath it. The two are not redundant; they answer different questions at different cadences.
